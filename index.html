Beyond the Classroom

I believe that true education happens as much on the sports field, the stage, and in the corridors of the boarding house as it does at the chalkboard.

I see my role as creating a safe, structured environment where young people learn the value of accountability, community, and self-respect.

High standards require high support.

Whether I am guiding a student through a difficult equation or a difficult personal decision, I am there to provide the firm boundaries they need to feel secure, and the relational warmth they need to thrive.

My Approach

Rigour & Clarity

By breaking down complex concepts into logical steps, I help students not only master the curriculum but develop the critical thinking skills they will use for the rest of their lives.

Community & Belonging

My goal is for every student to feel known, safe, and valued - understanding that their actions impact their peers. We celebrate diversity, demand mutual respect, and build lifelong bonds.

Guidance & Growth

Whether on the sports field or in a quiet one-on-one conversation, I challenge students to look beyond the immediate and envision who they want to become - grounded in integrity and driven by purpose.
