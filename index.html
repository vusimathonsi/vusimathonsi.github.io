Beyond the Classroom

I believe that true education happens as much in the corridors of the boarding house as it does at the chalkboard. As a Mathematics Educator, I demand rigour and clarity, teaching students that complex problems are solvable with patience and logic. As a Housemaster and Mentor, I translate that same approach into life skills - creating a safe, structured environment where young people learn the value of accountability, community, and self-respect.

My approach is built on a simple philosophy: high standards require high support. Whether I am guiding a student through a difficult equation or a difficult personal decision, I am there to provide the firm boundaries they need to feel secure, and the relational warmth they need to thrive.
