My Philosophy

I believe that academic performance begins with knowing the student. Trust breeds effort - when a student feels seen, they work hard for you, and eventually, they learn to work hard for themselves.

Read more

My 4 Pillars

Discipline

I raise questions, not my voice. True accountability comes from guiding students to examine their thinking and connect their actions to consequences. I enforce boundaries, but always with care.

Thinking Maths

I focus on the "why" and "when" of strategy over the "how" of procedure. Through open-ended questioning, I create a safe space for students to tackle difficult concepts without fear.

EdTech Solutions

I use digital tools to support teaching and learning, and not just for the sake of technology. Whether using GeoGebra to aid student understanding or writing custom scripts to automate my workflow. I build practical solutions.

Extra-curricular

By engaging with students on the sports field, stage and in the corridors, I build trust through shared experiences. This allows me to mentor the whole person.

The 5-Year Goal

Ultimately, I hope that a student under my guidance learns to lead with empathy, holds themselves to higher standards, and builds the habits that set them up for a lifetime of success.
